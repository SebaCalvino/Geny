About Tektra

Inspired by the Greek word téktōn (τέκτων), meaning "craftsman" or "builder"

"Creativity never sleeps, neither does Tektra"

Our Mission

Tektra solves the critical problem of creative opportunity loss due to overwhelming workloads and time constraints. While you're busy with essential tasks, Tektra continuously generates high-quality digital content - epic images, complete websites, and engaging games - ensuring you never miss a creative moment.

Our Vision

To be the autonomous creative engine that never stops working. We believe that creative potential shouldn't be limited by time constraints or heavy workloads. Tektra ensures your creative ideas are always being developed, even when you can't be there to work on them.

Key Features

Discover Tektra's unique capabilities

Epic Image Generation

Detailed prompts create unique and impressive visuals. Each image is saved with metadata for reproducibility.

Complete Websites

Responsive HTML/CSS/JS pages with coherent design, palettes and narratives. Includes landing page, navigation and footer.

Engaging Games

Beyond Snake: Tektra generates aesthetic and minimalist games with clear mechanics and victory conditions.

Organized Outputs

Creations are saved in `output/YYYY-MM-DD/` with an index for easy exploration.

Automation

Runs automatically on schedule via GitHub Actions, or locally with Flask.

API Endpoints

Check the latest creation with `/quehaces` and recent activity with `/novedades`.
